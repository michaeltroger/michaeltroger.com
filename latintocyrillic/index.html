Cyrillic Transliterator: Latin To Cyrillic

A Latin to Cyrillic and Cyrillic to Latin converter/transliterator.

Simple web version using the Serbian alphabet:

More alphabets and features in the Android version. With more than 50000 downloads one of the most successful apps of its kind, while being fully open source!

Included alphabets in the Android version:

  • Serbian Azbuka
  • Belarusian (ISO 9:1995)
  • Bulgarian (ISO 9:1995)
  • Macedonian (ALA-LC)
  • Macedonian (ISO 9:1995)
  • Russian (ISO 9:1995)
  • Ukrainian (ISO 9:1995)
