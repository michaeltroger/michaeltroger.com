Green Pass PDF Wallet

Stores and displays your most important PDF documents and their included QR codes like e.g. an EU Digital COVID PDF Certificate. Unofficial app that is fully open source. With ~150,000 downloads and shortly being one of the top 3 apps of Austria, this is my most succesful privately developed app so far.
